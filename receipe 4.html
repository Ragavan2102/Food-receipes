Tiramisu

A classic Italian dessert

Recipe Details

Ingredients:

  • 500g mascarpone cheese
  • 300ml heavy cream
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 150g sugar
  • 1 cup strong espresso, cooled
  • 2-3 tbsp rum or Marsala wine (optional)
  • Ladyfinger biscuits (Savoiardi)
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, beat egg yolks and sugar until creamy. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  3. In another bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture, then fold in the beaten egg whites.
  5. Mix the cooled espresso with rum/wine if using.
  6. Dip ladyfinger biscuits into the espresso mixture and line them in the bottom of a serving dish.
  7. Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture over the biscuits.
  8. Repeat the layers and finish with a dusting of cocoa powder on top.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight) before serving.
