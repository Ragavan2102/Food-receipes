Pasta Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish

Recipe Details

Ingredients:

  • 350g spaghetti
  • 150g guanciale or pancetta, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 75g grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 50g grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • Black pepper, freshly ground

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry guanciale/pancetta until crispy in a pan. Set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Pecorino Romano, Parmigiano-Reggiano, and black pepper.
  4. Once pasta is cooked, drain (reserve some pasta water), and transfer it to a warm serving bowl.
  5. Quickly toss the hot pasta with the egg-cheese mixture. The residual heat will cook the eggs and melt the cheese, forming a creamy sauce. Add pasta water if needed for consistency.
  6. Add the crispy guanciale/pancetta on top and serve immediately.
