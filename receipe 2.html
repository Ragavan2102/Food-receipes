Margherita Pizza

A classic Italian pizza

Recipe Details

Ingredients for Pizza Dough:

  • 500g strong flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 325ml lukewarm water
  • Olive oil

Ingredients for Topping:

  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, salt, and yeast in a large bowl. Gradually add water and knead until you get a smooth dough. Cover and let it rise for about an hour.
  2. Preheat oven to the highest temperature (usually around 475-500°F or 240-260°C).
  3. Divide dough into balls. Roll out into rounds on a floured surface.
  4. Place dough rounds on baking sheets. Spread tomato sauce on the dough, leaving space at the edges for the crust.
  5. Add sliced mozzarella and torn basil leaves on top. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
