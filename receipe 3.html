Risotto ai Funghi

A delicious Mushroom Risotto

Recipe Details

Ingredients:

  • 300g Arborio rice
  • 500g mixed mushrooms (porcini, cremini, shiitake), sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 liter vegetable or chicken broth, heated
  • 120ml dry white wine
  • 50g grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onions until translucent. Add garlic and cook for a minute.
  2. Add rice and stir until it's well-coated with oil. Pour in wine and cook until it evaporates.
  3. Add a ladleful of hot broth to the rice and stir frequently. Continue adding broth gradually as it gets absorbed by the rice. Cook for about 18-20 minutes until the rice is al dente.
  4. In another pan, sauté the mushrooms until golden. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stir in the cooked mushrooms and Parmigiano-Reggiano into the risotto. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
